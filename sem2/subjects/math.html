Engineering Mathematics-II

Syllabus

  1. Differential Equations - 1
    • Linear differential equations of second and higher order with constant coefficients by inverse operator method
    • Applications - initial and boundary value problems
    • SLE: particular integral for the functions of the type xn sinax/x
  2. Differential Equations - 2
    • Legendre’s differential equation
    • Method of variation of parameters
    • Series solution of differential equations
    • SLE: Cauchy’s differential equation
  3. Integral Calculus
    • Multiple integrals – evaluation of double and triple integrals
    • evaluation of double integrals over a region
    • Change of order of integration
    • Applications
    • Beta and Gamma functions – properties and problems
    • SLE: evaluation by changing into polar form
  1. Vector Calculus
    • Vector differentiation
    • Gradient, Divergence, Curl, Laplacian and their physical meanings
    • Solenoidal and Irrotational vectors - statement problems
    • Vector identities: curl(curl A) and div( A x B)
    • Vector Integration – Vector line integral
    • Green’s theorem
    • Stoke’s theorem
    • Gauss divergence theorem (without proof) – problems only
    • SLE: components of velocity and acceleration, div(curl A), curl(gradφ)
  2. Laplace Transforms – 1
    • Laplace transform of standard functions
    • standard properties
    • Periodic functions
    • Unit step function
    • Unit impulse function
    • SLE: Laplace transforms of discontinuous functions
  3. Laplace Transforms – 2
    • Inverse Laplace transforms
    • Convolution theorem(without proof)
    • solution of differential equations
    • simultaneous differential equations
    • SLE: Applications to engineering problems
